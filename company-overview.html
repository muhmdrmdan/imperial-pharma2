Company Overview

Manufacturing high bio-technological pharmaceutical solutions in Belgium for the MENA region.

Who We Are

A fully-integrated pharmaceutical company

Imperial Pharma is a fully-integrated pharmaceutical company operating in various therapeutic areas, with research, development and manufacturing capabilities in the Kingdom of Belgium, in addition to a dedicated marketing and sales scientific office.

We serve a local population of 100 million patients and are expanding to reach even more patients globally across the MENA region.

Two core divisions

Our core business relies on two main divisions — the Probiotic Division and the Anemia Division. We market a full range of probiotic finished products in the digestive, immunity and feminine health fields, in addition to anemia biotechnological products.

Manufacturing capability

Imperial Pharma works with overseas toll-manufacturing conglomerates in order to produce high bio-technological pharmaceutical solutions, held to strict Belgian and EU quality standards.
