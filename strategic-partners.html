Strategic Partners

We believe in technology transfer, partnering with leading overseas toll-manufacturers.

Imperial Pharma believes in technology transfer and works with overseas toll-manufacturing conglomerates in order to produce high bio-technological pharmaceutical solutions.

THT Probiotics

THT is a specialist in the manufacture of lactic acid bacteria and the custom formulation of innovative food supplements. Its production unit — certified ISO 9001 and ISO 22000 — is located in Belgium, south of Brussels, at the centre of Europe. Founded in 1991 by Professor Thonart of Université de Liège, THT later became the biotechnological branch of the Prayon group.

Organo Pharma

Organo Pharma is one of the leading pharmaceutical companies in the Egyptian market. Well established and owned by Dr. Mowfak Sakr since 2007, it offers a wide range of pharmaceuticals including antibiotics, anti-asthma, analgesics, antihypertensives, proton-pump inhibitors and more.

A fully-integrated pharmaceutical company developing high bio-technological probiotic and anemia solutions, manufactured in Belgium and trusted across the MENA region.
