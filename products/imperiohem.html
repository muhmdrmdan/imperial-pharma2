What is Imperiohem

Imperiohem is a bio-technology heme supplement that helps the formation of red blood cells and haemoglobin in cases where significant iron deficiency is experienced, delivering rapid management of iron status. It is recommended to restore iron and haemoglobin to normal levels.

Heme iron 18mgBio-technology sourceRapid iron management
Enquire about this product

Ingredients

Each Imperiohem tablet contains 18 mg of iron (fortified heme iron).

Indications

May be used in many situations associated with iron deficiency and low haemoglobin: female health, general surgery, gastroenterology, nephrology and cardiology; pregnant women requiring more iron; the elderly with poor diet; and growing children and youth whose increasing body and blood volume may lower bodily iron stores.
