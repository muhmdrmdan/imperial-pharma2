Lactobiotic Forte

Probiotic Range

What is Lactobiotic Forte

Lactobiotic Forte contains 4 strains of freeze-dried probiotic bacteria. When these good bacteria reach the digestive system they become re-activated and exert their positive effect on the gut flora. The probiotics are protected by a patented microencapsulation technology (DR Caps®) which guarantees superior survival and increases the quality and activity of the product.

Ingredients

Each Lactobiotic Forte capsule contains 5 billion freeze-dried germs: Lactobacillus rhamnosus, Lactobacillus acidophilus, Lactobacillus casei, Lactobacillus plantarum, Bifidobacterium animalis lactis, Saccharomyces boulardii and Bacillus coagulans. Excipients: corn maltodextrin and silicon dioxide.

Indications

Used in all cases where the gut flora needs extra help, such as irritable bowel symptoms (bloating, flatulence and soft stool). Lactobiotic Forte promotes good digestion and boosts intestinal flora after prolonged antibiotic therapy.
