Lactobiotic Ease

Probiotic Range

What is Lactobiotic Ease

Lactobiotic Ease contains 2 substances that are all the more active in the presence of flora imbalance: Inulin, a highly soluble prebiotic fibre that promotes the development of Bifidobacteria, and Bifidobacterium animalis lactis, one of the most studied probiotic strains in the world. It can be used by pregnant and breastfeeding women thanks to its natural composition, does not cause weight gain and can be used over a prolonged period.

Ingredients

Each sachet contains 5 billion freeze-dried germs: Bifidobacterium animalis lactis, Bacillus coagulans and 4g of Inulin as soluble dietary fibre. Excipient: silicon dioxide (anti-caking agent).

Indications

Used for all digestive discomforts associated with sensations of heaviness, bloatedness and flatulence, as it promotes good intestinal functioning and regular bowel movements.
