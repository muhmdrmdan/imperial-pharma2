Research & Development

Organic growth driven by developing new products and advancing existing ones.

R&D

The organic growth of our company is based on the development of new products, which is why one of the key links in our product life-cycle management chain is research and development.

The wide range of products across different legislative categories reflects the variety of knowledge and competencies within R&D. Our activities are equally focused on developing new products and advancing existing ones.

Product categories we develop

  • Prescription drugs (Rx)
  • Over-the-counter drugs (OTC)
  • Nutritional supplements

Together with experts from other business units, the R&D department is involved in technology transfers, clinical studies and other studies conducted during the development of each new product, to achieve and validate its quality, efficiency and safety.

A fully-integrated pharmaceutical company developing high bio-technological probiotic and anemia solutions, manufactured in Belgium and trusted across the MENA region.
