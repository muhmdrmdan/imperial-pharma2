Business Principles

Our positioning, vision, mission and the core values that guide everything we do.

Positioning

Passion for Better Health — the heart of everything we do.

Vision

We aim to improve quality of life by developing highly innovative pharmaceutical items and taking care of individuals' wellbeing, keeping their lives healthier and more energetic.

Mission

To implement the best business practices, meet the highest quality standards and provide outstanding performance, ensuring maximum value for our company stakeholders and end users.

Core Values

Five values, one direction

01

Innovation

We develop innovative pharmaceutical items that improve quality of life and make a positive difference to our customers' health.

02

Expertise

A selection of innovative, scientifically-evident active ingredients, conducted by an experienced R&D team.

03

Excellence

We encourage continuous learning and development, assume responsibility and take initiative to achieve extra value for us and our partners.

04

Proximity

Caring for each patient and offering effective solutions that make a positive difference to their health.

05

Teamwork

We share information openly and honestly with mutual respect, and build long-term partnership relationships built on trust.

A fully-integrated pharmaceutical company developing high bio-technological probiotic and anemia solutions, manufactured in Belgium and trusted across the MENA region.
